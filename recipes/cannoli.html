Cannoli Cream with Cookies

Description

Cannoli Cookies are made with all of the cannoli flavors you love in a light and fluffy ricotta cookie! Pistachios, chocolate and orange zest. Delicious end to a quality meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) container BelGioioso Ricotta Con Latte®
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons semisweet chocolate chips
  • 12 lemon-flavored shortbread cookies
  • 6 mint sprigs

Steps

  1. Slice chicken in half lengthwise to get thin pieces. Prick lightly using a fork or a knife to help tenderize.
  2. To serve, scoop a serving of the cannoli cream onto a dessert plate and garnish with cookies and a mint sprig.
